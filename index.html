Warm-It-Up

To warm-up we are going to do 1:00 of Cats and Cows. After that we are going to do 1 minute of reverse shoulder rolls. Next we are going to do 1:00 of inchworms.Then we are going to do alternate knee raises, after this we are going to have a rest for 30 seconds. After the reset we are going to do 1:00 of plank.Next we are going to do open-knee squating.After this we have right-arm plank for 1:00.We are now going to do Lunge Cal Stretches.We are going to go back to the plank but this time on the left arm.Now have a rest for 30 seconds. Finally we are going to go into bicycle kicks on our back.
